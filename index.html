What is Being Amma?

Being Amma is a cutting-edge mobile application designed to provide comprehensive support and Guidance to women during their pregnancy journey. Recognizing the unique needs and challenges that women face during this crucial period, Being Amma offers a holistic solution that combines pregnancy tracking, educational resources, diet and workout plans to ensure a healthy pregnancy experience.

Privacy Policy

Welcome to Being Amma! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect:

  • Personal Information: We collect your full name and email address solely for login and account authentication purposes.
  • Expected Delivery Date: This information is collected each time you log in to personalize your experience. We do not store this information after you log out.

2. How We Use Your Information:

  • To provide secure login access to the app.
  • To personalize content based on your provided expected delivery date during each session.

3. Data Storage and Security:

  • We do not store any personal data beyond your login session.
  • Your name and email are used strictly for authentication and are not stored for any other purpose.
  • We implement standard security measures to protect your information during use.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure:

  • We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal information to third parties.
  • We only use your data to enhance your experience within the app.

5. User Rights:

  • Since we do not store your data permanently, there is no personal data retained after you log out.
  • For any concerns regarding your login information, you can contact us directly.

6. Changes to This Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted within the app and on our website with an updated effective date.
